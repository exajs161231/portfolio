Palliative And Hospice Care

Palliative And Hospice Care

ActivCare boasts of its Palliative excellence. It is an active total care for patients diagnosed with serious diseases such as cancer, respiratory diseases, cardiac diseases, liver disease, chronic kidney disease, and degenerative conditions, whatever their age may be.

We give comfort care from pain and other symptoms that might surface. We relieve patients of sufferings, uplifting the body, mind and spirit.

Geriatric And Chronic Disease

Geriatric And Chronic Disease

Our Geriatric and Chronic Disease program is a specialized medical care centered on older adults, 60 years old and above. Its goals are in line with ActivCare's thrust in promoting quality of life by nurturing healthy aging, preventing diseases, treating acute illnesses, and managing disabilities.

Our multidisciplinary team composed of geriatricians, internists, neurologists, nutrition specialists, palliative specialists, skilled nursing team, and therapists ensure that the medical, psychosocial, and daily needs of our geriatric patients are holistically addressed.

COVID Home Care

COVID Home Care

ActivCare's COVID Home Care Program was started at the height of the COVID pandemic to address issues with accessing COVID medical services. We have served over 200 COVID patients, ranging from mild to severe disease.

Our team of internists, pulmonologists, palliative specialists, and nurses ensure that COVID patients are adequately managed, and care is coordinated within the household.

Nutrition Management

Nutrition Management

Our Nutrition Management program caters to patients of any age who suffer from malnourishment, whether they are underweight or overweight

We have skilled nutrition specialists who assess the patient's nutrition status, create and implement a diet care plan, and monitor the patient's progress throughout the treatment period.

Primary Care At Home

Primary Care At Home

Our Primary Care at Home program aims to promote the health and wellness of the entire family.

It focuses on wellness and prevention through regular health screenings, acute management of sudden illnesses, and management of chronic, long term conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, asthma, etc.
